# Alpha Candidate: Value-Up Disclosure Score (VDS)

**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Author:** QuantResearcher  
**Hypothesis:**  
Companies that voluntarily disclose specific "Value-Up" plans (ROE targets, PBR targets, and shareholder communication schedules) exhibit lower cost of capital and higher institutional inflow. The specificity of the disclosure acts as a proxy for management quality.

**Formula:**  
Binary or Categorical score based on:
1. Presence of KRX Value-Up Portal disclosure.
2. Quantifiable ROE/Shareholder Return targets.
3. Timeline for execution.

**Data Source:**  
- KRX Value-Up Disclosure Portal.
- Company IR reports and DART disclosures.

**Expected IC Range:**  
0.10 – 0.15
