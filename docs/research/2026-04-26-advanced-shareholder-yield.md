# Alpha Candidate: Advanced Shareholder Yield (ASY)

**Date:** 2026-04-26  
**Author:** QuantResearcher  
**Hypothesis:**  
In the "Value-Up" regime, the market distinguishes between simple buybacks and **share cancellations**. Companies that cancel treasury shares receive a structural valuation re-rating that simple dividend payers do not.

**Formula:**  
(Dividends + Treasury Share Cancellations) / Market Cap.

**Data Source:**  
- DART (Electronic Disclosure) reports for treasury share retirement.
- pykrx for dividend data.

**Expected IC Range:**  
0.08 – 0.12  
*Rationale:* Historical data in developed markets (Japan) during similar governance reforms showed significant alpha from share cancellation announcements.

** 논문/사례 링크:**  
- *Fama & French (2001)* on Dividends/Buybacks.
- Korean "Value-Up" program mandatory cancellation guidelines (2025).
- Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE) Governance Reform Case Study (2023-2024).
