## 1. Macro Context & Market Trend
Between October 2025 and April 2026, the South Korean stock market transitioned from a historic "super-bull" rally to a high-volatility, geopolitically sensitive regime.

*   **The 6,000 Milestone (Oct 2025 - Feb 2026):** Driven by an AI semiconductor supercycle and "Corporate Value-Up" reforms, the KOSPI broke 6,000 for the first time. Massive foreign inflows were attracted by tax incentives for dividends and mandatory treasury share cancellations.
*   **Geopolitical Shock (Mar 2026 - Present):** Conflicts in Iran and oil prices exceeding $100/barrel triggered a regime shift. Market sensitivity to energy prices and inflation has spiked, leading to a stabilization around the 6,400–6,500 range.

## 2. Impact on Quality top20 Strategy
*   **Edge Assessment:** The Quality top20 (ROE/GPA focus) has shown resilience during the March 2026 correction. While beta-heavy growth stocks plummeted, companies with strong cash flows and low debt have acted as defensive anchors.

*   **Recessionary Bear:** If high oil prices lead to a global slowdown, the current profitability (GPA) of export-oriented quality names may deteriorate rapidly.
