## 1. Macro Context & Market Trend
Between October 2025 and April 2026, the South Korean stock market transitioned from a historic "super-bull" rally to a high-volatility, geopolitically sensitive regime.

*   **The 6,000 Milestone (Oct 2025 - Feb 2026):** Driven by an AI semiconductor supercycle and "Corporate Value-Up" reforms, the KOSPI broke 6,000. 
    *   *Currency Factor:* KRW stayed in the 1,350 - 1,420 range, allowing for steady foreign carry-trade inflows.
    *   *Sector Rotation:* Extreme concentration in AI/HBM (Samsung/Hynix) and "Value-Up" leaders (Banks, Auto).
*   **Geopolitical Shock (Mar 2026 - Present):** Conflicts in Iran and oil prices > $100/barrel triggered a regime shift.
    *   *Currency Factor:* KRW spiked towards 1,480. Risk-off sentiment has stalled the "Value-Up" momentum in mid-caps.
    *   *Sector Rotation:* Pivot to Defense, Energy, and high-cash-flow Defensive Quality.

## 2. Impact on Quality top20 Strategy
*   **Edge Assessment:** The Quality top20 (ROE/GPA focus) has shown resilience. However, the edge is thinning as "low-beta quality" gets crowded during risk-off periods.

*   **Recessionary Bear:** Quality (ROE) is a trailing indicator. A prolonged high-oil environment will hit the GPA of export-dependent quality names in 6-12 months.
